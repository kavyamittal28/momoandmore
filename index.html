Our little story

Started with two hands, a bamboo steamer
and a really stubborn dream.

Momo & More began the way most good things do — a small kitchen, a bigger appetite, and a nagging feeling that the momos we grew up loving deserved a home of their own. So we opened one.

Every dumpling is folded by hand each morning. The chilli chutney is roasted, not blended. The chai is boiled, never steeped. And the playlist? Loud when it's a Friday and gentle when it's raining.

We're not a fancy place. We're a full one.
