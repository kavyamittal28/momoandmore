One year in · Just getting started

A small shop on HUDA Market
with a really full heart.

Cafe Momo & More opened its doors in Sector 23 with one idea — serve the juiciest momos in Gurgaon, and make people feel at home while they're at it. A year later, the feedback wall is running out of space.

Every dumpling is folded by hand each morning. The chutney is roasted, not blended. Post-its come free with every table. And somewhere between the tandoori momos and the churros with molten chocolate, we became a favourite haunt.

Serving happiness with a side of chutney & doodle vibes.
